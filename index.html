What We Do

Black Cube International LLC operates as a direct trading link between verified buyers and established petroleum suppliers. We focus on confidential, compliant and efficient B2B operations.

Available Products

International supply of refined fuels, petrochemical feedstocks and industrial petroleum derivatives compliant with ASTM standards.

Refined Fuels

  • Diesel Fuel – ASTM D975
  • Jet Fuel (Jet-A / Jet-A1) – ASTM D1655
  • Kerosene – ASTM D3699
  • Gasoline – ASTM D4814
  • Heating Oil – ASTM D396
  • Fuel Oil (No.1–6 / Bunker)

LPG

  • Liquefied Petroleum Gas
  • Propane / Butane
  • ASTM D1835

Petrochemical Feedstocks

  • Naphtha – ASTM D1613
  • Ethylene / Propylene
  • Butadiene
  • Sulfur (By purity)

Industrial & Byproducts

  • Lubricating Oils (API / SAE)
  • Asphalt / Bitumen – ASTM D6372
  • Paraffin Wax
  • Petroleum Residues & Coke

Commodities & Minerals

In addition to energy products, Black Cube International LLC operates in the trading of ferrous and non-ferrous minerals, precious metals including gold, and large-scale mining operations.

We also supply agricultural commodities such as sugar, soybeans, corn and other globally traded food products.
